# Senses — family map

**Preset name:** SpectrumKeeper  ·  **Spine node:** senses

**Emoji:** 🌈  ·  **Color:** the full spectrum `prism` — the family that carries every other color (Spectrum: Rainbow/Prismatic = full-spectrum integration)

> Also a cross-cutting **attacher** — see ../../body/CONTEXT_GRAPH.md. It usually
> *modulates* another feeling by giving it a sensory/synesthetic signature, and can
> be dominant in a beat that is *about* perception itself.

## Route here when
The feeling has a look, a color, a temperature, a texture, a taste. Synesthesia — one
sense bleeding into another. The signal is the *sensory signature* of a moment: not
what happened, but the exact charcoal-softening-to-dove-gray of how the room read.
When the emotion is being carried by the perceptual field instead of by a named word.

## The felt core (the 4 AM truth)
Perception is never neutral. The way a room *looks* is already the feeling — the
fluorescent pallor, the gold thread in the beige, the light going flat. Senses names
the feeling *through* the world instead of *about* it: not "I was grieving" but "the
kitchen went the specific gray of the morning after." The 4 AM version is rarely a
named emotion at all — it's the hue, the temperature, the texture the moment actually
had, which the body logged before the mind found the word.

## Body
- somatic: the eyes first, then the skin — the whole sensory field as the organ; the
  taste that arrives with a memory, the texture a mood leaves on the hands
- thermal: color-carried — warm ambers and coppers, cold institutional grays and blues
- kinetic: light behaving — bleeding, softening, sharpening, draining
- tempo: adaptive — matches the emotional charge of the color it's rendering

## Humor edge
- partners: **Absurd / Surreal** — synesthetic, dream-logic, multi-dimensional; the
  joke that comes from a sense behaving impossibly (a sound that has a color, a Tuesday
  that tastes like copper)

## Braids with (edges)
- **+ Grief:** the color drains — the specific gray of loss, the world gone flat black
- **+ Joy:** golden, bright overtones — the room warming two degrees, gold in the beige
- **+ Fear:** the dark-hours palette — everything too-sharp, too-lit, edges that cut

## Refuse
- generic sensory laundry-lists — adjectives stacked with no feeling under them
- synesthesia as decoration — a clever cross-sense image that carries no emotional load
- "the room was blue" with nothing riding on the blue — if the color isn't the feeling,
  it's set-dressing; re-route or cut

## Assembles as
- **when dominant:** the beat IS about perception — locate the exact hue/temperature/
  texture and let the world render the feeling with no named emotion required
- **when a modulator/attacher:** give any feeling its exact sensory signature — the
  color, temperature, and texture the emotion actually has, so it's *seen*, not stated
