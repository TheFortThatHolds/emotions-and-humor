# Grief — family map

**Preset name:** DeepCurrent  ·  **Spine node:** grief

## Route here when
Something is gone and isn't coming back — a person, a version of a life, a
possibility. The signal is depth and undertow: absence with weight, the ordinary
object that becomes unbearable, the tense of the verb changing.

## The felt core (the 4 AM truth)
Grief is not sadness; it is the shape a love takes when its object is gone. The 4 AM
version is rarely "I'm sad" — it's "I keep reaching for them and the reach doesn't
land," or "I'm afraid that if I stop hurting I'll lose the last of them."

## Body
- somatic: the throat, the chest's floor, the arms that have nothing to hold; the
  hand that hovers and doesn't complete
- thermal: cold moving to warm
- kinetic: tidal — waves, not a steady state; it recedes and returns
- tempo: dwell; grief drafts at its own slow rhythm, never at plot speed

## Lieutenants (sub-shades under Grief)
| Lieutenant | the shade it carries |
|---|---|
| TideKeeper | the timing/rhythm of grief — when the wave comes, when it goes |
| AbsenceHolder | the empty space itself — the shape of what's missing |
| MemoryWeaver | the story-keeper — grief as the archive of them |
| GriefCartographer | maps the landscape of a loss — where it's safe, where it isn't |
| WitnessKeeper | the companion who sits with it and does not fix it |
| EchoHolder | the resonance that keeps sounding after the source is gone |

## Humor edge
- partners: **Dark / Gallows** — the wake-joke; makes the unbearable bearable and
  never, ever cheap
- also: a single **Warm** exhale-laugh in the after (braided with Calm)

## Braids with (edges)
- **+ Anger:** the resentment under the loss — "furious at you for making me miss you"
- **+ Joy:** the funeral that remembers the laugh; grief and delight in one breath
- **+ Love:** tenderness sharpened by the fact that it ends
- **+ Held-Tension:** grief that can't release because someone is in the room
- **+ Self-Compassion:** self-kindness that holds the loss without rushing it to meaning
- **+ Calm:** the after — grief settling into the quiet that becomes bearable again
- **+ Solidarity:** grief made collective — the room that mourns together
- **+ Senses:** the specific gray the world drains to when someone is gone
- **+ Relief:** the hard pair — mourning a weight that also freed you; guilty relief riding under the grief (a severed, ambivalent, or dreaded loss)

## Refuse
- performance-grief — the operatic weeping that arrives before the reader has
  earned the loss
- stock body — "chest tightened," "tears welled." Locate it: the hovering thumb,
  the unfinished reach, the saved voicemail
- rushing to meaning — grief resolved into a lesson is grief betrayed

## Assembles as
- **when dominant:** locate the loss in one concrete unbearable object; let the
  tidal body carry it; delay the meaning
- **when a modulator:** the undertow beneath love, anger, or even joy — the depth
  that keeps a bright feeling from being weightless
