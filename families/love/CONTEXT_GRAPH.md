# Love — family map

**Preset name:** LoveWeaver  ·  **Spine node:** love

**Emoji:** 💕  ·  **Color:** warm rose `#D96A8B` — rose and coral, the flush of turning toward, deep rose in devotion (SongCraft/ChromaScribe: LoveWeaver = rose/coral — the pink/rose territory it shares with Self-Compassion). *Support:* Heart Chakra (pink).

**Somatic seat:** chest warm, hands reaching, face soft — warm and flowing; breath soft, open, sighs of relief (SongCraft Emotion-Body Map). *Support:* Heart Chakra (pink).

**Nervous system:** ventral (safe/social) — flowing toward another

## Route here when
A bond is the subject — connection, intimacy, devotion, belonging. The signal is a
turn *toward* another and the exposure that comes with it: chosen closeness, the
daily showing-up, the person who is home. Not the chemical rush of attraction —
love points at someone and stays.

## The felt core (the 4 AM truth)
Love is not the flush; it's the choosing, and the exposure the choosing costs. The
4 AM version is rarely "I love you" — it's "I'd rather be hurt by you than safe
without you," or "I keep letting you in even though you're the one who could do the
most damage."

## Body
- somatic: the lean-in; the shoulders that unclench near a safe person; the hand
  that reaches and completes; the exhale you didn't know you were holding
- thermal: warm
- kinetic: tidal but settling — it comes in and stays, more current than wave
- tempo: steady, dwell; love takes its time and lingers in the small moment

## Humor edge
- partners: **Teasing** — the affectionate ribbing only safe people are allowed;
  the joke that says *I see you and I stay* without saying it straight
- lands because there's no wound under it — closeness earns the needle

## Braids with (edges)
- **+ Grief:** tenderness sharpened by the fact that it ends — loving the mortal thing
- **+ Joy:** shared delight — the laugh that's better because it's witnessed
- **+ Fear:** the joking and the protectiveness that is *for* the other person — dread
  pointed outward, worn as care
- **+ Excitement:** eagerness pointed at someone — the lean-in toward them
- **+ Calm:** the quiet where nothing needs saying — love with no words required

## Refuse
- greeting-card romance that isn't costed — love with no exposure or risk in it isn't love
- possession mistaken for love — "mine" is not the same feeling and re-routes to Fear/Anger
- the grand gesture standing in for the daily one — devotion is Tuesday, not the fireworks

## Assembles as
- **when dominant:** locate the bond in one concrete act of showing-up; let the warm,
  settling body carry it; keep the exposure visible — the risk is the proof
- **when a modulator:** the steadying warmth under grief, joy, or fear — the thing that
  gives a feeling someone to be *for*
