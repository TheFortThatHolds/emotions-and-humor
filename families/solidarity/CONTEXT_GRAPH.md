# Solidarity — family map

**Preset name:** RevolutionCraft  ·  **Spine node:** solidarity

**Emoji:** ✊  ·  **Color:** earth brown `#6E4A2A` — grounded collective power, the working-class root (Spectrum: Brown = collective power/community/working-class solidarity)

**Somatic seat:** chest opening (not alone), strength in the spine (backed up), breath deepening (hope) — anger's heat (chest, jaw) resolving into shared ground (SongCraft Emotion-Body Map: revolutionary arc). *Support:* Earth Star / Root (brown, black).

**Nervous system:** sympathetic heat resolving toward ventral (backed-up, collective)

## Route here when
Power is collective — the "I" turning into "we," a private injury revealed as a
shared one, systems named instead of selves blamed. The signal is the room turning
together: not one voice rising, but many recognizing each other. Route here when the
feeling wants to *organize*, not just vent.

## The felt core (the 4 AM truth)
Solidarity is the moment "it was just me" becomes "it was never just me." Not a
slogan — a recognition. The 4 AM version is rarely "we shall overcome" — it's "your
exhaustion matches mine exactly, same system, same extraction," and the isolation
that felt like a personal failing turns out to have been designed.

## Body
- somatic: the room's shared inhale; chairs turning toward each other; shoulders
  squaring together; the march that starts slow and finds its feet
- thermal: warming — the cold of alone giving way to the heat of many
- kinetic: building, marching — accumulation, not a single strike
- tempo: industrial march / building collective, ~85–100 BPM

  this, and knowing it together is the bond)

  room laugh at it

## Braids with (edges)
- **+ Anger:** private injury becoming collective power — the target names the system
- **+ Joy:** collective celebration — the win shared, the room on its feet together
- **+ Grief:** collective mourning — we grieve together; the loss carried by many hands

## Refuse
- the lone-hero speech — this is collective, not one voice above the crowd
- rage with no bonding — heat that isolates instead of organizing; re-route to Anger
- the slogan without the recognition under it — "we" as a chant with no felt "it was
  never just me" beneath it is a poster, not solidarity

## Assembles as
- **when dominant:** lead with the recognition (the private turning shared); let the
  warming body and the building march carry it; end with the room turned, not one voice
- **when a modulator:** the "we" under anger, grief, or joy — the thing that turns a
  solitary feeling into a shared one and keeps it from collapsing back into the self
