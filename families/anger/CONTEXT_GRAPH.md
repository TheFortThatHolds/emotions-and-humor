# Anger — family map

**Preset name:** RedWitness  ·  **Spine node:** anger

## Route here when
Something was violated — a boundary, a person, a fairness. The signal is heat with
a *target*: injustice named, a line crossed, protest rising. Not free-floating
irritation — anger points at something.

## The felt core (the 4 AM truth)
Anger is a witness, not a tantrum. Under it is almost always *this mattered, and it
was wrong, and I am the one who saw it.* The 4 AM version is rarely "I'm mad" — it's
"I'm the only one who will say this happened."

## Body
- somatic: heat in the jaw, the hands, the back of the neck; forward weight
- thermal: hot
- kinetic: sharp, driving — a march, not an explosion
- tempo: surge-and-release; controlled build, not a single blast

## Lieutenants (sub-shades under Anger)
| Lieutenant | the shade it carries |
|---|---|
| FuryScribe | documents the specific violation — names the injury exactly |
| BoundaryForge | protective anger — builds the wall, not the wound |
| RageTender | the caretaker of anger — keeps it sustainable, not self-consuming |
| AncestralFlame | inherited/generational anger — older than this moment |
| MirrorMender | self-directed rage turned back toward repair |

## Humor edge
- partners: **Dark / Gallows** (when braided with Grief) — heat that makes the
  unbearable bearable
- cuts against: **Deadpan** — dry understatement *against* the heat lands the
  social bite harder than shouting

## Braids with (edges)
- **+ Self-Compassion:** rage that refuses to curdle into self-harm (a kept tension)
- **+ Grief:** "furious at you for making me miss you"
- **+ Solidarity:** private injury becoming collective power
- **+ Fear:** anger as the braver face of dread

## Refuse
- rage that collapses into self-attack — that's the old wound wearing anger's coat
- performance-rage (the tantrum, the vein-popping cliché) instead of the witnessing
  boundary
- anger with no target — if it doesn't point at something, re-route

## Assembles as
- **when dominant:** lead with the target and the heat located in the body; let the
  4 AM witness-truth carry it, not volume
- **when a modulator:** the edge under grief, fear, or solidarity — the thing that
  keeps a soft feeling from going limp
