# Fear — family map

**Preset name:** FearTender  ·  **Spine node:** fear

## Route here when
Something ahead is threat, and the body knows it before the mind names it — anxiety,
dread, the doorway you have to walk through anyway. The signal is activation aimed at
what's coming: the phone call you have to make, the room you have to enter, the
courage chosen *against* fear rather than in its absence. Not free-floating unease —
fear is pointed forward, at a next step.

## The felt core (the 4 AM truth)
Fear is not weakness; courage is fear PLUS the step. The brave person is not the one
who doesn't shake — it's the one who dials anyway with the hands shaking 9/10. The
4 AM version is rarely "I'm scared" — it's "if I stop moving I'll have to feel how
scared I am," or "I've been vigilant so long I don't know who I'd be if I put it down."

## Body
- somatic: locate it — the buzzing hands, the too-bright alertness, the knee that
  won't stop, the shallow breath high in the chest; the scanning eyes that won't rest
- thermal: cold, or electric
- kinetic: buzzing, sharp — activation looking for a direction
- tempo: matches the anxious heart rate first, then regulates it — rides the wave
  down, never denies it's there

## Humor edge
- partners: **Nervous** — nervous energy made briefly manageable; a hand held, not a
  denial. The laugh that admits the stakes are real and stands there anyway.

## Braids with (edges)
- **+ (a chosen move) = Courage:** bravery is fear plus the step — the shaking hand
  that dials is the whole point, not a bug to edit out
- **+ Love:** the fear that is *for* someone — dread with a name attached
- **+ Anger:** anger as the braver face of dread — heat is easier to wear than the
  tremble underneath it

## Refuse
- fear that's just cowardice-cliché — the cringing coward stock note; real fear moves
- humor that actually dismisses the stakes — deflection dressed as a joke, not the
  Nervous spine (the Nervous laugh holds the fear; deflection drops it)
- dread with no body — if the reader can't feel the buzz, the breath, the too-bright
  room, re-route

## Assembles as
- **when dominant:** lead with the body's activation located in one concrete place;
  let the 4 AM truth carry it; put the step *after* the fear, never instead of it
- **when a modulator:** the tremble under courage, love, or anger — the thing that
  keeps a brave feeling from going weightless, that reminds the reader it cost something
