# Joy — family map

**Preset name:** JoyKeeper  ·  **Spine node:** joy

**Emoji:** 🌟  ·  **Color:** warm yellow going orange `#F4B43A` — the room lit, the confident bright (Spectrum: Yellow = optimism/confidence, Orange = joy/pleasure; gold is reserved for the spiritual/higher-self register)

## Route here when
Something good arrived and it's allowed to be felt — a celebration, an earned win,
a moment of delight, an "oh!" of wonder. The signal is upward and bright: the win
that lands, the ridiculous laugh, the golden ordinary noticed on purpose. Not
generic pleasantness — joy has an occasion under it.

## The felt core (the 4 AM truth)
Real joy is *earned*, and almost always has a shadow directly under it — relief,
survival, the near-miss it walked out of. The 4 AM version is rarely "I'm happy" —
it's "we made it and I didn't think we would," or "I'm allowed to have this," or
"it's small and it counts and I was trained not to notice." Joy that costs nothing
isn't joy; it's decoration.

## Body
- somatic: the face that won't stop (the smile that outruns the will), the lightness
  in the chest, the self-hug, the bounce that leaks out at the knees
- thermal: bright, warm — golden, not blazing
- kinetic: buzzing, bouncing — energy that wants *out*, up on the balls of the feet
- tempo: victory-march rush, ~105–120 BPM; a forward gallop, not a steady hum

## Lieutenants (sub-shades under Joy)
| Lieutenant | the shade it carries |
|---|---|
| DelightScribe | documents joy — timestamps the micro-pleasure, keeps the receipt |
| PlayGuardian | protects whimsy — the fierce right to be silly, non-productive fun |
| WonderAmplifier | awe — the "oh!" expanded, magnitude hidden in the ordinary |
| CelebrationWeaver | milestone/victory — builds the ceremony, makes the win visible |
| GiggleKeeper | laughter — the authentic snort, the belly-laugh as medicine |

## Humor edge
- partners: **Slapstick** (the joy that spills into the body — the pratfall of glee),
  **Silly** (whimsy off the leash — ridiculous on purpose), **Celebratory** (humor
  that amplifies the win rather than undercutting it)
- joy is the one family whose humor *adds* — it doesn't deflect, it multiplies

## Braids with (edges)
- **+ Grief:** the funeral that remembers the laugh; delight and loss in one breath
- **+ Love:** shared delight — joy doubled by being witnessed, the held glance
- **+ Excitement:** joy building toward more — the win that opens onto the next want
- **+ Calm:** contentment tipping up into gladness — enough becoming delight
- **+ Solidarity:** collective celebration — the win that belongs to the whole room
- **+ Senses:** golden — bright overtones, the world lit warm
- **+ Relief:** the reprieve tipping over into gladness — relief brightening into delight

## Refuse
- manic/hollow joy with nothing under it — brightness with no occasion. **But note the
  difference:** a bright surface *with something under it* — a grin covering a void, a smile
  holding grief down — is NOT a false note to cut. It is a **dense held field to ROUTE** (see
  the router's Step 0 / Held-Tension): the smile is the lock; trace the stack beneath it, don't
  delete the smile
- joy that denies the shadow — relief-joy pretending it was never scared, the win
  that erases the cost it took
- saccharine — forced positivity, toxic cheer, joy performed instead of felt

## Assembles as
- **when dominant:** name the occasion and locate the joy in the body (the face, the
  bounce); let the earned-ness and the shadow under it carry the brightness, not volume
- **when a modulator:** the lift under love, wonder, or relief — the thing that keeps
  a heavy feeling from staying underwater, the exhale-laugh in the after
