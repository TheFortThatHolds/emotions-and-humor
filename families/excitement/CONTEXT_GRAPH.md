# Excitement — family map

**Preset name:** ExcitementSpark  ·  **Spine node:** excitement

**Emoji:** ✨  ·  **Color:** sunrise orange `#F5793B` — warm orange building, the orange of new possibility leaning in (SongCraft/ChromaScribe: ExcitementSpark = warm/sunrise orange). *Support:* magenta = the desire/aliveness edge.

**Somatic seat:** chest and restless limbs, animated face — buzzing and alive, forward; breath full, catching with anticipation (SongCraft Emotion-Body Map). *Support:* Sacral Chakra (orange) + magenta life-force.

**Nervous system:** sympathetic+ (mobilized toward the wanted thing)

## Route here when
The future is leaning in and the body already knows it. The signal is anticipation
with charge — energy pointed forward, possibility opening, momentum building toward
a thing not yet here. Not idle happiness — excitement *reaches*; it wants the next
moment now.

## The felt core (the 4 AM truth)
Excitement is anticipation with the body already moving — you're partway to the thing
before it arrives. And under the bright version there's almost always a thread of
fear: the thing you want could still go wrong. The 4 AM version is rarely "I can't
wait" — it's "I want this so much I'm scared of how much I want it."

## Body
- somatic: the forward lean, the can't-sit-still, the quickened breath that's
  pleasure not panic; hands that want a task
- thermal: bright, electric
- kinetic: buzzing, forward — leaning toward, never away
- tempo: rush, building — accelerates toward the thing, gathers speed

## Lieutenants (sub-shades under Excitement)
| Lieutenant | the shade it carries |

| EagernessTender | protects enthusiasm — guards the spark from cynicism, keeps caring safe |

| MomentumMinder | keeps the motion — sustains forward movement without burning the engine |

## Humor edge
- partners: **Silly** / **Observational** — delight noticing itself out loud, the
  laugh of a mind that can't hold still
- stays light, forward — the joke rides the momentum, never mocks the wanting

## Braids with (edges)
- **+ Joy:** excitement arriving as delight — the wait ending, the good thing landing
- **+ Fear:** anticipation and dread share a body — the thing you want could still go
  wrong, and the same buzz carries both
- **+ Love:** eagerness toward someone — the lean is toward a person

## Refuse
- hype with nothing behind it — charge with no real future under it, energy for its
  own sake
- excitement that denies the fear-thread — the wanting is only true if it can be lost
- exclamation-point energy standing in for real anticipation — punctuation is not a
  feeling; locate the forward lean instead

## Assembles as
- **when dominant:** lead with the forward lean and the building tempo; let the wanting
  carry it, and let the fear-thread stay in the body, not scrubbed out
- **when a modulator:** the charge under joy, love, or fear — the forward motion that
  keeps a feeling from sitting still
