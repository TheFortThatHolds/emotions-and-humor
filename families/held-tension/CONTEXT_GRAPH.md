# Held-Tension — family map

**Preset name:** ClenchKeeper  ·  **Spine node:** held-tension

> Also a cross-cutting **body attacher** — see ../../body/CONTEXT_GRAPH.md. It can be
> dominant (a beat about the cost of composure) OR braid into any *observed* feeling.

## Route here when
Composure is holding under pressure that cannot drop — the all-day performance, the
held face in a room that can't be left. The signal is the *cost* of keeping it
together: the body bracing where nobody sees, a grip that won't release in public.
Not the feeling on display — the muscle paying for it underneath.

## The felt core (the 4 AM truth)
Held-Tension is the composure tax, paid below the belt where nobody asks. The 4 AM
version is rarely "I'm stressed" — it's *"my face was fine all day, my shoulders held
the line, and something deeper was paying the bill."* The muscle holding the whole
performance together where every other muscle is already on display.

## Body
- somatic: the deepest muscle — pelvic-floor bracing, the deep-core grip; the jaw and
  shoulders that held the line; the trapped breath below the diaphragm that never
  reached the floor; the whole-body unclench that takes twenty minutes
- thermal: tight, cold
- kinetic: locked, still-under-pressure — holding as a literal physical act, not metaphor
- tempo: held all day, then a long slow release — released ONLY when unobserved

## Humor edge
- partners: **Deadpan** — the dry face worn as the public mask, the even joke over the
  clench (the working-body one-liner that keeps the composure intact)
- otherwise humor is usually held too — the brace doesn't let the laugh all the way out

## Braids with (edges)
- **+ Grief:** grief that can't release because someone is in the room
- **+ Fear:** dread held behind a calm, even face
- **+ Anger:** rage swallowed to keep the job — held heat with nowhere to go
- **+ Body-Awareness:** the release *out of* the clench — the opposite it finally meets

## Refuse
- dramatizing the release as a breakdown — it's a body finally *allowed*, not a collapse
- releasing while still observed — the grip only lets down unwatched; break that and the
  whole truth of it breaks
- medicalizing or joking past the somatic — name it the way the body already knows it

## Assembles as
- **when dominant:** the beat IS the cost of composure and the private release — locate
  the held muscle, honor the brace, let the unobserved unclench be the climax
- **when a modulator/attacher:** brace any observed feeling — the grip under a held face;
  release only when unobserved
