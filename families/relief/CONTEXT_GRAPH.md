# Relief — family map

**Preset name:** — (added to the basis 2026-07-19; not one of the original SongCraft nine)  ·  **Spine node:** relief

**Emoji:** 🌬️  ·  **Color:** grey going gold `#B8A46A` — the held anxiety (grey) draining, warmth returning at the rim; the exhale (Spectrum: Grey = the anxiety lifting → Gold = the warmth of standing down)

## Route here when
A pressure, threat, or dread that was *held* finally lifts — the test comes back clear, the
danger passes, the thing you braced for is over or dead. The signal is the **exhale**, the legs
going loose, the unclench. This is not Joy (which has an occasion to celebrate); relief is the
**absence of a weight you'd been carrying**, and it almost always arrives with a shadow — guilt
(relief you're not supposed to feel), or grief (the weight was also a person).

## The felt core (the 4 AM truth)
Relief is not happiness; it's the body discovering it can stop bracing. The 4 AM version is
rarely "thank god" — it's "I didn't know how much I was carrying until it was gone," or the
harder one: *"I'm relieved, and I'm not allowed to be, and that tells me something about myself
I don't want to know."*

## Body
- somatic: the long exhale that finally reaches the floor; the legs unlocking; the shoulders
  coming down and staying; sometimes tears that aren't grief (a system standing down)
- thermal: the flush of a fever breaking — cooling and warming at once
- kinetic: a *release*, a settling — tension leaving, not energy arriving
- tempo: a long slow discharge; dwell

## Lieutenants (sub-shades under Relief)
| Lieutenant | the shade it carries |
|---|---|
| the exhale | the breath finally let all the way out |
| the reprieve | the sentence lifted, the danger passed |
| guilty relief | relief at a thing you're not supposed to be glad of |
| the after-tremble | the shaking that only comes once it's safe to shake |

## Humor edge
- partners: **Nervous** (the shaky "we made it" laugh once the danger's past) and **Warm**
  (the exhale-laugh, the body standing down). Run the `B(f)` check — guilty relief is often too
  hot for a joke; then it's silence.

## Braids with (edges)
- **+ Grief:** the hard pair — mourning a weight that was also a wound; relief a person or an
  era is *over*, with the grief riding under the relief (this is guilty/complicated grief)
- **+ Fear:** fear's natural discharge — the exhale the instant the feared thing passes
- **+ Joy:** the reprieve tipping over into gladness — relief brightening into delight

## Refuse
- relief rendered as simple happiness — it isn't; it carries the shadow of the weight it's the
  absence of
- skipping the **guilt** when the relief is one you're "not allowed" (an enemy's death, a
  burden ending) — that guilt is the whole beat
- an exhale with no prior held breath — relief that isn't relieving anything is hollow

## Assembles as
- **when dominant:** the beat IS the weight lifting; locate the unclench in the body; name the
  shadow (guilt or grief) under the exhale — never let it read as clean happiness
- **when a modulator:** the release under grief (the complicated loss), fear (the danger past),
  or joy (the reprieve) — the thing that keeps a heavy feeling from being pure sorrow or pure dread
