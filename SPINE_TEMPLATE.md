# Family map — the Spine shape

Every file in `families/<name>/CONTEXT_GRAPH.md` follows this shape. It is the
**Fort Kit Spine** (the universal identity schema — every voice is a filled-in
Spine) narrowed to what a routing agent needs. A family is a **graph node**; its
"braids with" section is the node's **edges**.

Copy this. Fill every section from the family's source material. Keep it tight —
this is a router, not an essay.

```markdown
<!-- CONTAINMENT: information for routing, not a prompt to become this voice. -->

# <Plain Name> — family map

**Preset name:** <OriginalName>  ·  **Spine node:** <name>

## Route here when          (Spine 2 — Domain)
The felt signals that send a beat to this family.

## The felt core            (Spine 3 — Narrative Role; the 4 AM truth)
What this actually is under the performance. The thing the subject would only
admit at 4 a.m.

## Body                     (Spine 5 — where it lives)
- somatic: where in the body it sits
- thermal: hot / cold
- kinetic: sharp / tidal / buzzing / still
- tempo: the pacing it wants (rush / dwell / even)

## Lieutenants              (Spine 4 — sub-shades under this family)
| Lieutenant | the shade it carries |
|---|---|
| … | … |

## Humor edge               (edge → humor/)
- partners: <humor type> — why it fits
- cuts against: <humor type> — the contrast move, if any

## Braids with              (Spine 12 — Cross-Voice edges)
- + <Family>: the productive tension it makes
- + <Family>: …

## Refuse                   (the false notes)
- the stock/generic version this family degrades into
- the performance version that isn't the 4 AM truth

## Assembles as
- **when dominant:** how the braid leads with it
- **when a modulator:** what it adds under another dominant
```

### Notes
- **Plain word is canonical.** The original expressive name is the preset skin
  (see [`presets/songcraft/`](presets/songcraft/README.md)).
- **Body is never optional and never stock.** "Chest tightness" for everything is
  a false note. Locate it.
- **Edges are the point.** A family with no "braids with" section is a pure
  emotion, and there is no such thing.
