# Hook (craft node)

**Preset name:** HookForge

Distills a complex feeling into a **memorable, repeatable core** — the two words
that carry twenty. Used by songs (the chorus hook), and by any surface that needs a
title, a headline, or a line that sticks.

## Principles
- find the two or three words that hold the whole braid
- repetition that *deepens* meaning, not just repeats it
- test for the involuntary "yes" — does it land in the body?

## Examples
- "THE FORT I BUILT" — a whole trauma-to-strength arc in three words
- "WE REMAIN" — collective endurance as power, in two

## Refuse
A hook that's catchy but hollow — sound with no feeling under it. The hook must
compress a *real* braid, not replace one.
