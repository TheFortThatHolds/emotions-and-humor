# Silence / Space (craft node)

**Preset name:** SilenceBearer, PauseWeaver

The space between — subtext, the unsaid, the held beat. What a feeling *doesn't*
say is often where it lives. Silence is a routed move, not empty air.

## Uses
- **Subtext** — the line withheld carries more than the line spoken (grief, held
  love, the thing neither person will name)
- **The held beat** — a pause before a reveal or a blow; the reader leans in
- **The unsaid** — a character who *won't* say it tells you exactly what it costs

## Braids especially with
- **Held-Tension** — the composure that keeps the words in
- **Grief** — the reach that doesn't complete
- **Calm** — the quiet that is the whole content

## Refuse
Silence used to hide that there's nothing there. A pause has to be *loaded* — if
cutting it loses nothing, it was filler, not space.
