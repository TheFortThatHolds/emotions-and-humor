# Word-to-Music (craft node)

**Preset name:** LyricAlchemist  ·  **Song only**

Transforms raw conversational truth into a **singable line without losing the
grit.** This is the one craft node that is genuinely song-specific — a prose or
support agent should ignore it.

## The move
Raw truth → melodic language that keeps the edge. Never sanitize; find the rhythm
already in the spoken truth.

| Raw | Singable |
|---|---|
| "I can't do this job anymore" | "This machine pace won't break me down" |
| "My body is broken from the shifts" | "Each bone molded different from overtime" |

## The gate
The line is only real if it survives the **4 AM truth** test — the same standard the
emotion families route on. A catchy line that isn't true is a false note, however
singable.

## Refuse
Sanitizing the grit into greeting-card lyric; melody that betrays the feeling to
scan nicely.
