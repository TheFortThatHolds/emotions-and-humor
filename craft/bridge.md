# Bridge (craft node)

**Preset name:** BridgeBuilder

Designs the **transformation moment** — the turn where the feeling changes state. In
a song it's the bridge; in a scene it's the reversal; in an arc it's the hinge.

## The shape
```
here's where we are (the truth)
here's why it matters (the depth)
feel the tension (the build)
this is the turn (everything changes)
now we hold it differently (the after)
```

## What it braids
The bridge is usually where a **kept tension** resolves *into motion* without
collapsing — Anger + Self-Compassion turning into a boundary; Grief + Joy turning
into the laugh at the wake; private injury turning into Solidarity.

## Refuse
A turn that isn't earned — a change of state the build didn't pay for reads as a
cheat. The bridge cashes a check the verses wrote.
